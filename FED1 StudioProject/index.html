ABOUT US

Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry is a British wizarding school, located in the Scottish Highlands. It accepts magical students from Great Britain and Ireland for enrolment. It is a state-owned school, funded by the Ministry of Magic.






While attending Hogwarts, students take field trips to Hogsmeade. Hogsmeade Village, or simply called Hogsmeade, is the only all-wizarding village in Britain. It was founded by Hengist of Woodcroft. Since before 1714, Hogwarts third years and above have been permitted weekend trips into the village.




There are many extracurriculars at Hogwarts with the most popular being Quidditch. Quidditch is a fast, dangerous, exciting game in which two teams flying on brooms compete for points scored by throwing a ball – the Quaffle – through hoops on either end of a large grassy pitch.
